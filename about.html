The Hertz Story

  • Student started up in 2015 when we noticed the passion and craze people possessed towards cars and touring especially with the Ferrari.

  • Ever since, Student, has been true to its core beliefs in building a driving community across the globe with highly maintained cars.

  • We never believed in competition hence opened the doors for others by procuring the first license in South India. Unlike others we also do not carry just one make or        
	model, all our locations feature a wide variety appropriate for exploring, touring and commuting.
  • Our locations are a one stop solution for all your driving needs. Our brand is built on 2 words – ‘Quality’ and ‘Brotherhood’. We do not provide cars but an experience to 
	cherish.
  • Our motto, ‘We do not compete, we care’ is evident from the growing number of Franchise with us. All our locations share the same enthusiasm and excitement to give 
	the customer an amazing experience. 
    • 
	
  • From the start to the end of your trip, Student will work around the clock to give you an experience to remember.

A word from the founders

	
 "The major hurdle was to get an official nod from the RTO to operate self-ride cars as these never even existed and there was no provision in their online system to 
	   incorporate one. The process like any other, was affected largely by delays and running around. When this seemed like an impossible task, Mr.Ravi Kumar, came 
       	   as a life saver, who took up our case by himself. Greatly appreciating the uniqueness and potential of the start-up, Mr.RaviKumar speeded up the formalities and other 
           documentation that was required for the self-ride licence to be given to Student. His intention as a bureaucrat was to encourage entrepreneurship in young minds 
	   and to support innovative ideas which could help our country in the future. Since then, there was no looking back and Student became the first car rental start-up 
           to get such a licence in South India..
